Voice

Serious, humane, and concrete. Instructional copy says you; the origin story says I. Sentence case everywhere; uppercase lives only in tracked-out mono labels. Concepts anchor to an era or a machine, and the numbers are real: 1-bit, 4 colors, 16 × 16. No emoji. Unicode does the pointing: → · ×
