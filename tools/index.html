All Tools

Every interactive exercise in one place, tagged by the concept it makes visible.

The tools are not decorations around the curriculum. They are the curriculum's method: a way to turn hidden rules, old constraints, and graphics pipeline math into something a student can manipulate before they are asked to name it formally.
