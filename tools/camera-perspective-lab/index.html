Unit 4 — Camera and Perspective

Camera & Perspective Lab

Move the camera, change the projection, and watch the same scene become a different image without changing the objects.

The camera is a transformation system. It decides where the viewer stands, what the viewer looks at, and how depth is flattened onto the screen.
