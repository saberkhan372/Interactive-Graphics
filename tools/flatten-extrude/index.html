Unit 2 — 2D to 3D

Flatten/Extrude

Start with a flat shape. Add depth. Rotate it. The third dimension becomes a choice you can see instead of a word you memorize.

Extrusion makes the third dimension concrete. A front face is copied backward, matching vertices are connected, and the side faces appear as a consequence of that operation.
