Assignments

The real project briefs from both years of the course, merged into one canonical version each. Original due dates kept as historical record.

The assignments carry the same argument as the tools: constraint first, then expression. Each one names its requirements plainly, keeps a bonus list for students who want to push, and ends on the student's own site.
